## DATA COLLECTION

### What We Collect

Quantum Falcon collects minimal data necessary for software functionality:

- **License Keys**: Stored encrypted locally on your device
- **User Preferences**: Stored locally in your browser
- **Trading Statistics**: Stored locally for display purposes

### What We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:

- Your exchange API keys (stored locally only)
- Your private wallet keys
- Personal identification information (unless you provide it)
- Trading history or transaction details
- Financial account information

## DATA STORAGE

### Local Storage

All sensitive data is stored **locally on your device**:

- License keys are encrypted using device-specific keys
- API keys are stored in browser local storage
- Trading data remains on your device

### No Server-Side Storage

We do **NOT** store your data on our servers. All data remains on your device.
